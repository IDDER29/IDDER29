## Thesis

Software is not lines of code. It is the last mile of a decision about what should exist and why. My job is to close that distance — from concept to running system, from spec to shipped product, from bug report to deploy.

I build full-stack. I think in systems. I care about the craft.

---

## What I Build

### For businesses
Revenue-generating web experiences. Shopify storefronts that convert. React/Next.js applications that scale. The kind of work where the business outcomes are measurable and the code is maintainable by anyone who comes after me.

### For humans
Products people actually use. [Social Compass](https://github.com/IDDER29/social-compass-mobile) is a campus super-app with real users — events, ticketing, community. Not a demo. Not a portfolio piece. A product.

### From scratch
When I was at 1337 (the 42 Network school in Agadir), I rebuilt the C standard library by hand. A shell. A printf. A sorting engine. Not because I needed to — because understanding what's beneath the abstraction makes everything above it clearer.
